The Best Movies According To Tyler

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

1. Everything All At Once

I thought this was a very interesting watch. The story and themes are very thought provoking and just the whole premise had me intruiged.

2. Sinners

The music in this movie is amazing and all the inspiration, actors, and just the fresh feeling it gives is great.

3. Nope

I know people did not like this movie but I really enjoyed the premise as well and the acting. The alien in the sky and how the people chose to deal with it is fun to think about.
